Occupational Therapist

  • Job Title: Occupational Therapist (OT)
  • Job Location: Orlando, Florida
  • Status: Part-time, Full-time

Job Description

Job Overview:

This position is responsible for providing outpatient rehabilitation services within a home-based setting, focusing on the functional needs of the older adult population. The in-home setting could be a patient's private residence or in a patient's room in a Dementia-care facility, in an assisted living community, or in an independent living community. This position will evaluate and treat in the most functional environment which is the patient's home and assess the need for, develop, and deliver occupational therapy interventions to facilitate rehabilitation both on an intermittent and maintenance-based scale.


Duties:

  • Screens, examines and evaluates patients, including history and systems review
  • Ensure that the physician's orders are appropriate and discuss necessary changes.
  • Evaluate the effectiveness of occupational therapy intervention and update the patient care plan, as needed.
  • Assist in development, and implementation, of the interdisciplinary patient care plan.
  • Communicate with the physician, on a regular basis, regarding patient's status, obtaining additional orders, if necessary.
  • Instruct the patient in prescribed therapeutic self-care and creative activities that are directed toward improving independence.

Requirements:

  • Possess reliable transportation for commuting to the clinic.
  • Home Health or Hospice experience
  • Degree in Occupational Therapy from an accredited institution.
  • Strong clinical assessment and diagnostic skills.
  • Ability to lift and assist patients as needed
  • Ability to make appropriate clinical judgments.
  • Good written and oral communication skills; interpersonal communication and teaching skills.
  • Excellent interpersonal skills and a compassionate approach to patient care.

  • Salary Range: $38.43 - $57.65 per hour
  • Expected hours: 8 hour shift
