Medical Social Work

Overview

If you need assistance with community resources, you may be referred to a Medical Social Worker (MSW) for all your medical needs. Your Medical Social Worker will be responsible for assisting the patient and their families with understanding the various resources Linil Visiting Nurses, Inc. provides.

How We Serve You

Our Medical Social Workers services include:

  • Short-term counseling
  • Referral to aid programs and community agencies
  • Assistance with living arrangements
  • Financial assistance
  • Long-range planning

Meet with a Consultant

After deciding what service is best suited for you, you can schedule an appointment with one of our consultants to ask any other questions you may have about the service and the admission process.
