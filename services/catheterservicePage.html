Catheter Care

Overview

Our Catheter Care services are managed by a Registered Nurse (RN) or Licensed Practical Nurse (LPN) with training and experience for the treatment. They will be responsible with educating you about the proper medical equipment usage and how to clean and dispose of waste.

How We Serve You

Catheter Care services include:

  • Education with the cleaning and disposal process
  • Equipment and supply management

Meet with a Consultant

After deciding what service is best suited for you, you can schedule an appointment with one of our consultants to ask any other questions you may have about the service and the admission process.
