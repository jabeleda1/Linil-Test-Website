Enteral Therapy

Overview

Our Enteral Therapy services are provided by a Registered Nurse (RN) or Licensed Practical Nurse (LPN) with training and experience in providing enteral treatment at the home. Your nurse will communicate frequently with your physician to update your plan of care. They will also be responsible for your initial evaluation and re-evaluations for care.

How We Serve You

Enteral Therapy services:

  • Wound Care
  • Education with the disease process
  • Teaching self-care techniques
  • Developing prevention strategies to keep you out of the hospital

Meet with a Consultant

After deciding what service is best suited for you, you can schedule an appointment with one of our consultants to ask any questions and develop a plan for your specific care needs.
