Medical Social Services

We here at Linil Visting Nurses, Inc. provide a wide range of quality services to help those in need of dependable healthcare. From Therapy-to-Nursing, these services we bring are delivered with the utmost kindess and care to meet your home care needs.

Medical Social Work

Our Medical Social Workers will work closely with patients and their families to provide them with information about programs, community agencies, financial assistance and long-range planning.

Learn More Schedule Consultation

Home Health Aide

Our Home Health Aides will monitor the condition of the patient and assist them with any daily life activities they are experiencing struggle with at home. These include patients with disabilites or chronic illness.

Learn More Schedule Consultation

Companion Care

Our Companion Care service will involve providing the social and emotional support for the patient at home to improve mental health and well-being. This includes hosuekeeping and meal preparation.

Learn More Schedule Consultation

Personal Care Assistant

Our Personal Care Assistants will provide assistance to patients who are unable to perform daily activities due to a disability or illness and overall improve quality life changes. These quality life changes include daily tasks like walking, feeding, and bathing.

Learn More Schedule Consultation

Respite Care

Our Respite Care services provide temporary relief for family caregivers to comfortably step away for a short-term period of time to take a break from their duties and rest or travel. In the meantime, the service will take care of all the patients needs.
