Therapy Services

We here at Linil Visting Nurses, Inc. provide a wide range of quality services to help those in need of dependable healthcare. From Therapy-to-Nursing, these services we bring are delivered with the utmost kindess and care to meet your home care needs.

Physical Therapy

Our Physical Therapists will work alongside you to evaluate and develop a personalized plan based on your specific physical diagnostic. This includes treatments like general home exercises and providing equipment to ease mobility and prevent further pain or injury.

Occupational Therapy

Our Occupational Therapists will work alongside you to evaluate and develop a personalized plan based on your injury, illness, or disability. This includes treatments like maintaining skills needed for daily living and demonstrating certain exercises for pain relief.

Learn More Schedule Consultation

Speech Therapy

Our Speech Therapists will work alongside you to evaluate and develop a personalized treatment plan to help improve communication skills or speech disorders. This includes treatment like mouth exercises and swallowing techniques.
