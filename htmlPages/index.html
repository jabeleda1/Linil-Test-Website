Linil: Home Care from the Heart

Linil Visiting Nurses, Inc. is a Medicare-Certified and CHAP-Accredited Home Health Agency serving the
Greater Central Florida area. We help people recover from illness, injury or surgery, avoid hospitalization,
and manage chronic conditions so that you stay safe and independent in your own home.

COMPASSION & SERVICE

With Linil, you can expect to receive comprehensive
and compassionate care in the comfort and safety of
your own home.

Since 2002, we have been serving the Central Florida
area and providing extraordinary home health care to
seniors in Orlando and its surrounding area, including
Winter Park, Apopka, Altamonte Springs, Casselberry,
Winter Springs, Winter Garden, Ocoee, Oviedo, Sanford,
Lake Mary and much more.
