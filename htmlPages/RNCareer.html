Careers at Linil

Registered Nurse

  • Job Title: Registered Nurse (RN)
  • Job Location: Orlando, Florida
  • Status: Part-time, Full-time

Job Description

Job Overview:

We are seeking a compassionate and dedicated Registered Nurse to join our healthcare team. The ideal candidate will possess a strong commitment to providing
high-quality patient care, particularly for individuals with developmental disabilities. This role requires a blend of clinical expertise, effective communication
skills, and the ability to work collaboratively within a multidisciplinary team. The Registered Nurse will be responsible for assessing patient needs, developing
care plans, and implementing appropriate nursing interventions.


Duties:

  • Wound Care
  • Wound VAC
  • IV Treatment
  • Conduct comprehensive assessments of patients health status and needs.
  • Administer medications and treatments as prescribed by healthcare providers.
  • Educate patients and their families about health conditions, treatment options, and preventive measures.

Requirements:

  • Ability to Commute
  • Home Health or Hospice experience
  • 3 years relevant experience.
  • College Graduate or Bachelors Degree.
  • Able to follow policies and procedures and provide detailed documentation.
  • Ability to make appropriate clinical judgments.
  • Good written and oral communication skills; interpersonal communication and teaching skills.
  • Excellent interpersonal skills and a compassionate approach to patient care.

  • Salary Range: $72,000 - $84,760 per year
  • Expected hours: 20 - 40 per week
