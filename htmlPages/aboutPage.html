About Linil

Linil's History


Linil Visiting Nurses, Inc. was started by nurses with many years
of clinical experience. To this day, our Agency continues to be
operated by clinicians and medical providers with extensive
experience in many clinical settings - hospitals, rehabilitation
facilities, and home healthcare. We proudly serve Orange,
Seminole, Osceola and Brevard counties.

Our Mission

We provide patient-focused, comprehensive and high-quality home health services to the communities
we serve in a compassionate manner that exceeds their individual clinical needs, affirms their
dignity and independence, and reinforces our commitment to integrity and positive citizenship.

Our Exceptional
Employees


Rest assured, that all employees at Linil Visiting Nurses, Inc.
from your nurse to our secretaries - are thoroughly interviewed
and screened through several regulatory agencies, including the
Agency for Healthcare Administration, Florida Department of
Health, and Florida Department of Law Enforcement.

Additionally, we have a multi-lingual professional staff fluent in
Spanish, French Creole and Tagalong.
