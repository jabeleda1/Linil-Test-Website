About Linil

Linil's History


Linil Visiting Nurses, Inc. was started by nurses with many years of clinical experience. To this day, our Agency continues to be operated by clinicians and medical providers with extensive experience in many clinical settings - hospitals, rehabilitation facilities, and home healthcare.

We proudly serve Orange, Seminole, Osceola and Brevard counties.

Our Mission

We provide patient-focused, comprehensive and high-quality home health services to the communities we serve in a compassionate manner that exceeds their individual clinical needs, affirms their dignity and independence, and reinforces our commitment to integrity and positive citizenship.

Our Exceptional
Employees


Rest assured, that all employees at Linil Visiting Nurses, Inc. from your nurse to our secretaries are thoroughly interviewed and screened through several regulatory agencies, including the Agency for Healthcare Administration, Florida Department of Health, and Florida Department of Law Enforcement.

Additionally, we have a multilingual professional staff fluent in Spanish, French Creole and Tagalog.

Our Values

Accountable

Our employees are accountable for their own actions and must always act with professionalism when representing the agency.


Honest

Our employees will act openly and truthfully to maintain the highest degree of trustworthiness when serving patients, their family and caregivers.

Saftey-Focused

We will work to ensure potential vulnerabilities for saftey are addressed immediately, proactively and collaboratively.


Patient-Focused

The sanctity of a patient's home as well as their active involvement in their own care improves the healing process and maximizes independence.

Open

Our business and transactions will be conducted with transparency, openness and honesty, with only the highest level of integrity.


Excellent Service

All care, treatment and services provided will exceed the expectations of patients, family members, caregivers, physicians, other healthcare providers and the community-at-large.
