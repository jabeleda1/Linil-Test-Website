Linil: Home Care from the Heart

Linil Visiting Nurses, Inc. is a Medicare-Certified and CHAP-Accredited Home Health Agency serving the Greater Central Florida area. We help people recover from illness, injury, surgery, hospitalization and manage chronic conditions so that you can stay safe and independent in your own home.

Medically-Certified


Linil Visiting Nurses, Inc. is Medicare-Certified and has been accredited by the Community Health Accreditation Program (CHAP) since March 7th, 2013.

Trusted and Dependable
Homecare Service


Since 2002, we have been serving the Central Florida area and providing extraordinary home health care to seniors in Orlando and its surrounding area.

These locations include Winter Park, Altamonte Springs, Casselberry, Winter Springs, Winter Garden, Ocoee, Oviedo, Sanford, Lake Mary and much more.

THERAPY SERVICES

We offer Physical, Occupational, and Speech Therapy Services. All of our therapists serve as part of your interdisciplinary care team. Your care team is in constant communication, and provides each other with updates on how you are doing and reports changes of your progress and health.

Read More Schedule Consultation

NURSING SERVICES

Our skilled nursing services are provided by a Registered Nurse (RN) or Licensed Practical Nurse (LPN) with training an exprience in providing care in the home. Your nurse will communicate frequently with your physician to update your plan of care. They will also be responsible for your initial evaluation and re-evaluations for care.

Read More Schedule Consultation

MEDICAL SOCIAL SERVICES

We offer other services such as Medical Social Worker (MSW), Home Health Aides (HHA), Companion, Personal Care Assistance, and Respite Care to meet any of your home care needs. Anytime you receive services from an aide or assistant, they will be supervised by one of your nurses or therapists.
